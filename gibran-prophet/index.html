The Prophet

Kahlil Gibran · 1923

Almustafa, the chosen and beloved, prepares to depart from Orphalese after 12 years. As he waits for his ship, the people gather and ask him to speak his truth. In 26 discourses — on love, marriage, children, work, joy and sorrow, and more — he delivers his timeless wisdom before sailing home.

“Work is love made visible.”
